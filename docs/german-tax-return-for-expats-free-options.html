Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to file a tax return in Germany as an expat?
Yes, as an expat in Germany, you are required to file a tax return annually, regardless of your income level. The German tax authority, Finanzamt, offers free resources and support to help you navigate the process.

What is the deadline for filing my German tax return?
The deadline for filing your German tax return is typically July 31st of each year, but it's essential to check with the Finanzamt or a tax professional for specific deadlines and requirements.

Can I use a free tax filing service like Check24 for my German tax return?
Yes, services like Check24, Taxfix, or Wundertax offer free or low-cost options for filing your German tax return. These services can guide you through the process and ensure you take advantage of all eligible deductions and credits.
