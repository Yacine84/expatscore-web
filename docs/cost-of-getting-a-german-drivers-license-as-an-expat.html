What is actually happening

The high cost of getting a German driver's license is largely due to the theoretical and practical exams required by law. Expats must take a minimum of 12 theoretical lessons and 12 practical driving lessons with a licensed instructor, which can be costly. Furthermore, the cost of getting a German driver's license as an expat can be affected by the need to open a German bank account, such as a blocked account, to pay for the required lessons and exams.
