Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my BahnBonus points balance?
You can check your BahnBonus points balance by logging into your account on the Bahn website or through the Bahn app. Ensure you have your login credentials ready, and if you encounter any issues, contact BahnBonus customer support.

Can I use my BahnBonus points for any DB train?
While BahnBonus points can be used for various DB trains, there are specific rules and restrictions, especially for ICE tickets. Always check the BahnBonus website for the most current information on redeeming points for your desired travel.
