What is actually happening

The C1 medical language course is a requirement for foreign doctors in Germany, as it ensures that they have the necessary language skills to communicate effectively with patients and colleagues. The course is designed to assess the doctor's ability to understand and use medical terminology, as well as their ability to communicate complex medical information. To enroll in a C1 medical language course, foreign doctors can contact institutions such as the Goethe-Institut or the Deutsche Akademie für Sprache und Literatur.
