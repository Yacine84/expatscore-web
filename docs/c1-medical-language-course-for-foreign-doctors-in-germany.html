What is actually happening

The C1 medical language course is a prerequisite for foreign doctors in Germany, as mandated by the Federal Ministry of Health. This course is designed to ensure that international medical professionals have the necessary language skills to practice medicine safely and effectively in Germany. The course typically covers topics such as medical terminology, patient communication, and German healthcare systems. To enroll in a C1 medical language course, you'll need to provide proof of your medical degree and language proficiency.
